Established in 1947, the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is a cornerstone in the world of computing and technology. Its extensive membership, including academics, researchers, practitioners, and students, fosters idea exchange and best practices.

ACM-W is an initiative within ACM supporting and advocating for women in computing, providing networking, mentorship, and professional development opportunities.

SIG-AI, a segment of ACM, is a community focused on the realm of artificial intelligence. It provides a forum for students to collaborate and advance the field of AI.

SIG-SOFT, a part of ACM, is a community dedicated to software engineering. It serves as a platform for students to collaborate and advance the field of software engineering.
