JOIN US

Interested in joining ACM as a student member?

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you seamlessly obtain your ACM student membership.

Step 1 : Visit https://www.acm.org/ and click on the "Join" button located at the top right corner of the page.

Step 2 : If you are an Indian student, click on the "India" link under the Students section. Otherwise, select the option that suits your region.

Step 3 : Fill in the required details, scroll down, and click Continue to proceed.

Step 4 : If you are interested in additional resources such as ACM Books Series, Special Interest Groups (SIGs), Journals, Transactions, or Magazines, select the respective options on the checkout page to add them to your cart.

Step 5 : Choose your state of residence, click on "Complete using Indian CC", complete the payment process, and take a screenshot of the confirmation page.
